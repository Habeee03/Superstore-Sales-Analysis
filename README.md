# Superstore Sales Analysis

The primary aim of the sales analysis dashboard designed in Power BI, is to provide real-time, visual insights into sales performance, enabling data-driven decisions to boost revenue, optimize strategies, and track goals. 

### Some key insights from the Dashboard are highlighted below:

- ✅ Total Sales: $14.92M. Sales increased by 33.23% vs previous year which means the company generated significantly more revenue compared to the previous year.
- ✅ Total Profit: $2.62M. Profit decreased by 76.56% vs previous year, which is a major  concern.
- ✅ Order Quantity: 215K orders. Order quantity dropped to 98.08% vs previous year, indicating a sharp decline in recent purchasing activity.
- ✅ Total Loss: -$1.10M. This confirms that a large portion of transactions or regions are generating losses.
- ✅ Central region is the overall best performing region generating $4.6M in sales and $807k in total profit.

### Overall, the dashboard reveals an important pattern: 

The company is achieving strong sales growth and high order volumes in key regions such as Central and states like California, but profitability is declining due to significant losses occurring in certain states and cities. This suggests that while demand remains strong, there may be issues related to pricing strategies, operational costs, or product performance in specific locations that need to be addressed to improve overall profitability.
